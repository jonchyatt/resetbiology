## Phase Details

### Phase 6: Production Reconciliation & Contract Foundation
**Goal**: Members receive deterministic, historically faithful protocol assignments from the real production route, with the versioned identities and reviewed safety metadata needed by every later workflow.
**Depends on**: Nothing (first v2.0 phase; v1.0 is complete)
**Requirements**: FND-01, FND-02, FND-03, FND-04, FND-05, FND-06, FND-07, FND-08, FND-09, FND-10, FND-11, FND-12
**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE):
  1. A member can assign REHIT and receive the correct named six-week, three-session-per-week journey, while two other live protocols also produce correctly sized named plans without reseeding or rewriting production documents.
  2. A returning member sees one deterministic active assignment, and can inspect the original versioned prescription after protocol or exercise metadata changes without duplicate, ambiguous, or fabricated history.
  3. Member-day history remains correct across midnight and time-zone changes, and comparable exercise identity distinguishes movement variant, equipment, and units before prior targets are reused.
  4. The live `/workout` experience—not its dead route shadow—passes the inherited schema, 390-pixel header, dropdown contrast, tone/dead-code, and two-line REHIT-title checks while preserving existing feedback, points, readiness, and local-day behavior.
  5. Exercise purpose and minimum reviewed safety metadata are available before downstream compression, freshness, or swaps, and public copy does not present the unsupported 13%/3% claim or other audited overstatements as fact.
**Plans**: 06-01 control-plane establishment (no FND requirement credit); runtime plans remain pending ownership release
**UI hint**: yes

### Phase 7: Repeat-Safe Logging & Event Spine
**Goal**: Members can record a set with almost no interruption and trust that offline storage, retries, reloads, and account changes will never duplicate or misattribute accepted work.
**Depends on**: Phase 6
**Requirements**: LOG-01, LOG-02, LOG-03, LOG-04, LOG-05, LOG-06, LOG-07, LOG-08, LOG-09, LOG-10, LOG-11, LOG-12, LOG-13, LOG-14
**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE):
  1. A comparable straight set opens with an honest prefilled target, can be accepted in one tap or adjusted, and immediately offers auditable Undo plus a ±15-second deadline-based rest timer that never blocks early logging.
  2. Accepted and pending work survives refresh, crash, weak connectivity, suspension, lost responses, and multi-tab replay without creating a second set, transition, point award, or receipt; a changed reuse of an event identity is rejected.
  3. Logging remains partitioned to the authenticated member across logout and account changes, and when durable browser storage is unavailable the member sees an honest online-only state before any unacknowledged action is described as saved.
  4. Before the writer is enabled, an inspected production uniqueness rule and authenticated canonical read-back prove one accepted event and original replay receipt under a simultaneous race.
  5. During logging, a member can use an accessible unit-aware plate calculation, reviewed demonstration and form/stopping cues, and one optional final-set effort check without that check being required or framed as medical clearance.

### Phase 8: Guided Strength & Honest Manual REHIT
**Goal**: Members can complete, reduce, safely stop, or deliberately resume guided strength and manual REHIT sessions while the system records exactly what was delivered.
**Depends on**: Phase 7
**Requirements**: RUN-01, RUN-02, RUN-03, RUN-04, RUN-05, RUN-06, RUN-07, RUN-08, RUN-09, RUN-10, RUN-11, RUN-12, RUN-13, RUN-14, RUN-15
**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE):
  1. A strength session states its finite contract, keeps the current and next action clear, and lets a member move among full, reduced, express, or recovery variants while preserving accepted sets and explicitly recording intended versus delivered dose.
  2. A member who exits early or returns after reload, crash, or a large clock jump sees neutral partial-work and deliberate resume/discard choices and is never dropped directly into hard work.
  3. An eligible member can run the complete manual REHIT sequence only after reviewed contraindication, stopping, alternative, and cue-preflight gates; the player names the compatible modality and never claims smart-bike control, power measurement, maximal-effort verification, or hardware equivalence.
  4. REHIT and rest timing remains deadline-correct through backgrounding and sleep, with complete silent text/visual cues, optional audible final-three-second cues, reduced-motion behavior, a reachable stop, and an honestly degrading wake lock.
  5. A completed session pulses and toasts once, advances once, awards the existing 40 workout points once, and informs tomorrow’s guidance without claiming that a consumer score measured recovery.

### Phase 9: Readiness Prescriptions, Freshness & Activity
**Goal**: Members receive a visible, deterministic same-day prescription from readiness data and can understand estimated freshness and activity context without mistaking either for medical truth.
**Depends on**: Phase 8
**Requirements**: RDY-01, RDY-02, RDY-03, RDY-04, RDY-05, RDY-06, RDY-07, RDY-08
**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE):
  1. Scores at 39, 40, 69, and 70 produce the correct recovery, reduced, or ready prescription, visibly preserving or changing the assigned session according to deterministic protocol rules.
  2. A member can inspect the original plan, adjusted plan, exact difference, reason, input snapshot, and rule version; before Phase 10, readiness can change dose but cannot silently replace an exercise.
  3. Recovery guidance cannot be overridden by points, continuity, streak, or coach pressure, and a member can always choose to stop.
  4. The front/back freshness map explains the logged-set, movement, elapsed-time, and readiness inputs behind each estimated region, while the activity path shows planned rest, observed load, adjustments, and missing data as unknown rather than zero.
  5. Readiness, freshness, and activity copy consistently describes planning estimates—not medical clearance, injury prediction, measured recovery, or proof of fitness.

### Phase 10: Purpose- and Safety-Aware Substitutions
**Goal**: Members can choose reviewed exercise alternatives that preserve the plan’s purpose, respect equipment and readiness, and route pain or injury language away from generated advice.
**Depends on**: Phase 9 (using the metadata foundation from Phase 6 and event spine from Phase 7)
**Requirements**: SWAP-01, SWAP-02, SWAP-03, SWAP-04, SWAP-05, SWAP-06
**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE):
  1. An eligible exercise offers two or three clinically reviewed alternatives filtered by movement purpose, program role, equipment, skill, readiness, joint demands, and contraindications—not by same-muscle matching alone.
  2. A member can see whether a replacement preserves or changes the session purpose before choosing it.
  3. Nonclinical reasons such as equipment or preference become inspectable, resettable preference events, while pain or injury language always produces the fixed stop-and-professional-referral path before ranking or generation.
  4. A confirmed swap updates the current and next actions without duplicating accepted work and preserves both exercise identities in history; fair-trial copy stays neutral and the swap remains available.

### Phase 11: Evidence, Progress, Habits & Shared Nutrition Scorecard
**Goal**: Members can understand training progress, function, protein support, weight context, and adherence through confidence-bearing evidence shared consistently across workout and nutrition.
**Depends on**: Phases 9 and 10
**Requirements**: EVD-01, EVD-02, EVD-03, EVD-04, EVD-05, EVD-06, EVD-07, EVD-08, EVD-09, EVD-10, HAB-01, HAB-02, HAB-03, HAB-04, HAB-05, HAB-06, HAB-07, HAB-08
**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE):
  1. The primary progress view presents an evidence ladder across training exposure, comparable performance, function, protein coverage, and weight context, with every estimate showing provenance, recency, coverage, comparability, missing inputs, rule version, and a plain-language confidence explanation.
  2. A member can inspect normalized and raw strength anchors, chair-stand and optional grip trends, and known confounders without any view claiming tissue measurement, diagnosis, or false precision when data is missing or equipment changes.
  3. Workout and nutrition render the same versioned monthly scorecard from their respective owned facts, agree at local-day boundaries and missing-data cases, and export or share a receipt only after explicit member action.
  4. Protocol rings or timelines, the local-day month calendar, and detail drill-down show accepted session outcomes, readiness, and awarded points; full, reduced, express, recovery, and planned rest all preserve neutral weekly continuity.
  5. Express sessions, bounded weekly forgiveness, break/vacation mode, private 7/30/100-session milestones, and deterministic weekly insight help a member continue without shame, leaderboards, body comparison, or clinical-outcome claims.

### Phase 12: Unified Workout Coach & Safety Fences
**Goal**: Members can use one familiar workout coach for bounded education and proposals, with fixed code safety rules and fresh confirmation preventing unsafe or hidden actions.
**Depends on**: Phase 11
**Requirements**: COACH-01, COACH-02, COACH-03, COACH-04, COACH-05, COACH-06, COACH-07, COACH-08
**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE):
  1. The workout dock uses the approved coach personality and one swappable model/endpoint boundary, grounded only in the authenticated member’s training log, readiness, assigned protocol, and reviewed evidence.
  2. Pain or injury, medication or dosing, eating-disorder, and compulsive-exercise signals receive fixed reviewed safety responses before any model call, and generated anatomy, form, injury assessment, medication advice, or hidden programming never appears.
  3. A member can inspect and freshly confirm or decline a narrow proposed log, mode change, or reviewed swap; nothing writes before confirmation, and a confirmed action uses the same ownership and repeat-safe receipt path as a direct action.
  4. Educational-not-medical-advice text remains visible, provider, quota, retry, and rate-limit failures degrade gracefully, and quality logging excludes raw transcripts unless a separate retention and access decision is ratified.

### Phase 13: Craft, Accessibility, Telemetry, Security & Production Proof
**Goal**: Members can use the complete engine accessibly and privately on production, while independent evidence proves the exact release, its data cleanup, and its least-privilege credential state.
**Depends on**: Phase 12
**Requirements**: UIA-01, UIA-02, UIA-03, UIA-04, UIA-05, UIA-06, TEL-01, TEL-02, TEL-03, SEC-01, SEC-02, SEC-03, SEC-04, SEC-05, OPS-01, OPS-02, OPS-03, OPS-04, OPS-05, OPS-06, OPS-07, OPS-08, OPS-09, OPS-10
**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE):
  1. On production at 390 pixels and 200% zoom, loading, empty, retry, player, timer, ring, map, chart, dialog, and form states have no horizontal overflow, preserve layout, expose reachable 44-by-44-pixel targets where practical, and work with keyboard, visible focus, screen-reader announcements, and real numeric labels.
  2. Normal-motion, reduced-motion, muted-audio, low-bandwidth media, caption/description, contrast, and color-independent cue paths all remain complete in the shared portal visual language.
  3. Production telemetry reports real-path tap and continuation outcomes using only allowlisted coarse fields; it contains no loads, repetitions, exercise names, pain text, readiness answers, medication data, health notes, or raw coach transcripts.
  4. Production reads and writes succeed through the new least-privilege environment-backed database credential, no secret appears in any artifact, and a fresh isolated attempt proves the revoked old credential can no longer authenticate.
  5. From clean commit-bound work and run-scoped test identities, an independent blind verifier can reproduce all ten end-to-end scenarios, canonical ownership/read-back and exact baseline cleanup, zero console errors, dual-eye frame/timecode verdicts, and an audio-bearing cue receipt, with every deviation and Karpathy build/debug result recorded.
