# Reset Biology Vision GODMODE — Project

## Mission

Build a guided vision gym people want to use for 60 sessions. The therapeutic variable and the member-facing score are visual thresholds, not reaction time or arcade skill.

## Priority law

The general-adult contrast and near-vision experience is the trunk. It receives the deepest work and closes before integration, convergence, art, coach, or headset branches can take scope from it.

## Product promise

Reset Biology trains the visual brain to process low-contrast, blurred, and crowded detail more efficiently and reports performance on the trained tasks. It does not claim to reshape the eye, reverse lens hardening, cure refractive error, diagnose disease, or replace professional amblyopia or convergence care.

## Existing foundation to preserve

- Three-tab vision surface and 12-week curriculum.
- Six binocular modes, phase gates, enrollment, streaks, points, and untimed mode.
- Guided runner, engine registry, proof/report flow, metrics persistence, charts, weekly assessment, first-session preview, and recovery/safety surfaces.
- Tester traversal through `testDayOffset`, with the saccade drill pinned.
- Gabor library engine, reminders, and 24 supplement placements in weeks 3–12.

## Non-negotiable engineering laws

- True-DPR procedural/vector therapeutic stimuli.
- Threshold-controlled difficulty and forced-choice decisions.
- Quiet detection zones; beauty at edges, transitions, and reward beats.
- One hard therapeutic session per member-local day.
- Client-only camera processing; calibration scalars only in per-device storage.
- Code-enforced symptom stops and honest-claim fences.
- Additive database/API evolution and never-strip regression proof.
- Git-push deployment only; Vercel CLI is banned.
- Production dual-eye proof for every visual gate.
